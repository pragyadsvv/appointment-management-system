Take a look of our hospital

Pushpanjali Hospital & Research Centre, Pvt. Ltd. Is incorporated under the Companies Act as Private Limited Company to extend the medical facilities to the communities at large residing in the vicinity of Agra and 200 K.M. from Agra. We have all highly advanced facilities and qualified & trained nursing staff in all wards having 350 beds including ICCU, CCU, PICU, NICU, Emergency, Trauma and BURN Unit (with a provision of expansion up to 450 beds). We are extending exceptional range of technology combined with remarkable spectrum of clinical expertise at very competitive rates under one roof through a team of more then 100 specialists & super specialists for all specialized and general treatment.
